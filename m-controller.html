Overview

A flexible control center for distributed gas detection.

The M-Controller brings remote gas transmitters, analog sensors, alarms, and control outputs into one programmable system. Its architecture supports installations ranging from mechanical rooms and parking structures to refrigeration plants and industrial facilities.

The controller can display gas readings, evaluate alarm conditions, activate local or remote relays, operate horn and strobe outputs, and make system information available to external automation platforms.
